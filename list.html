Mathematics Roadmap

Your guided path to mathematical mastery

1. Logic & Set Theory Foundation

  • Propositional Logic
  • Predicate Logic
  • Basic Set Operations
  • Functions & Relations
  • Counting Principles (Basic Combinatorics Introduction)

2. Number Theory & Combinatorics Intermediate

  • Divisibility, Prime Numbers, and Modular Arithmetic
  • GCD, LCM, Euclidean Algorithm
  • Fermat's and Euler's Theorems
  • Permutations & Combinations
  • Binomial Theorem
  • Pigeonhole Principle
  • Inclusion-Exclusion

3. Algebra Intermediate

  • Polynomials & Factorization
  • Exponents & Logarithms
  • Linear & Quadratic Equations
  • Systems of Equations
  • Sequences & Series (Arithmetic & Geometric)
  • Complex Numbers (Basic Operations & Polar Form)
  • Matrices & Determinants (Basics for solving equations)

4. Inequalities & Functional Equations Advanced

  • Cauchy-Schwarz, AM-GM, Jensen's Inequality
  • Solving Functional Equations (Algebraic and Non-Algebraic)

5. Geometry & Trigonometry Intermediate

  • Euclidean Geometry (Congruence, Similarity, Circles)
  • Coordinate Geometry (Equation of Line, Distance, Midpoint, Section Formula)
  • Trigonometric Identities & Equations
  • Law of Sines & Cosines
  • Vectors (Dot & Cross Products, 3D Coordinate Geometry)

6. Graph Theory Advanced

  • Graph Representation (Adjacency Matrix/List)
  • Eulerian & Hamiltonian Paths
  • Trees & Spanning Trees
  • Graph Traversal (DFS, BFS)
  • Shortest Path Algorithms (Dijkstra, Floyd-Warshall)

7. Calculus Advanced

  • Limits & Continuity
  • Differentiation & Applications (Maxima, Minima, Curve Sketching)
  • Integration (Definite & Indefinite)
  • Differential Equations (First Order & Higher Order)
  • Multivariable Calculus (Partial Derivatives, Gradient, Divergence)

8. Probability & Statistics Intermediate

  • Basic Probability Rules & Conditional Probability
  • Bayes' Theorem
  • Random Variables & Distributions (Binomial, Poisson, Normal)
  • Expectation & Variance
  • Hypothesis Testing & Confidence Intervals
  • Regression & Correlation
