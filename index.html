What math subject does your child need help with?

With advanced degrees in mathematics from top schools around the country including the Ivy League, our team of accomplished tutors is ready to help your child today with any of these subjects.

Algebra

Linear equations, quadratic functions, polynomials, and systems of equations.

Calculus

Limits, differentiation, integration, and differential equations.

Geometry

Euclidean geometry, areas, volumes, and geometric transformations.

Statistics

Descriptive statistics, hypothesis testing, and regression analysis.

Probability

Basic probability rules, conditional probability, and Bayes' theorem.

Trigonometry

Trigonometric functions, identities, and the unit circle.

Number Theory

Prime numbers, modular arithmetic, and Diophantine equations.

Combinatorics

Counting principles, permutations, combinations, and the binomial theorem.

Matrices

Matrix operations, determinants, eigenvalues, and linear transformations.

Sequences & Series

Arithmetic and geometric sequences, infinite series, and convergence tests.

Complex Numbers

Complex arithmetic, polar form, and De Moivre's theorem.

Inequalities

AM-GM inequality, Cauchy-Schwarz, and solving inequality problems.

Graph Theory

Graph representation, traversal algorithms, and path problems.

Logic & Set Theory

Mathematical proofs, propositional logic, and set operations.

Vectors & Coordinate Geometry

Vector operations, dot and cross products, and 3D coordinate geometry.

The benefits of working with International Math Hub

Better grades

It's no secret that tutoring pretty much always helps improve grades in the long term. We also focus on short-term improvements to show quick improvement if college applications are around the corner.

Improved retention

We focus on understanding the principles behind the math, so the actual algebra, factorization, you name it, becomes the easy part. This helps promote a much more lasting understanding that underpins all future math subjects.

Foundation for success

When a child gets the bigger picture, it's easier to see how it all fits together. That means that they perform better in the near term and are set up for future success since they're starting farther along than their peers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our tutoring services

How do I schedule a tutoring session?

You can schedule a session by contacting us through our contact form, calling us directly, or using our online booking system. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs.

What qualifications do your tutors have?

All our tutors hold advanced degrees in mathematics or related fields from top universities. They have extensive teaching experience and undergo regular training to ensure the highest quality instruction.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes, we offer both online and in-person tutoring sessions. Our online platform provides the same high-quality instruction with the convenience of learning from anywhere.

What subjects do you cover?

We cover all mathematics subjects from elementary school through college level, including algebra, geometry, calculus, statistics, trigonometry, and more specialized topics.

How do you track student progress?

We use comprehensive progress tracking that includes regular assessments, homework completion rates, test scores, and detailed reports shared with both students and parents.
