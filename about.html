About International Math Hub

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind the platform.

Our Mission

At International Math Hub, our mission is to empower students and educators by providing accessible, high-quality resources that bridge educational gaps. We aim to support learners preparing for Mongolia's General Entrance Exam while fostering a global community of math enthusiasts.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student has access to the tools and guidance they need to excel in mathematics. By integrating innovative features and culturally relevant content, we strive to create a learning platform that resonates globally while addressing local needs.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Ensuring our resources are available to all learners.
  • Collaboration: Building a global community of math enthusiasts.
  • Innovation: Incorporating cutting-edge tools and methods for effective learning.
